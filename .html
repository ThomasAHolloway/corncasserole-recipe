Corn Casserole

This easy and delicious Corn Casserole is a crowd-pleaser made with just a few ingredients. It's creamy, slightly sweet, and perfect for any potluck, holiday dinner, or weeknight meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 (15 oz) can whole kernel corn, drained
  • 1 (15 oz) can creamed corn
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup (1 stick) melted butter
  • 1 box Jiffy corn muffin mix (8.5 oz)
  • Optional: 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Preparation Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine whole corn, creamed corn, sour cream, melted butter, and Jiffy mix.
  3. Mix well until all ingredients are fully incorporated.
  4. Pour the mixture into a greased 9x9 inch baking dish.
  5. If desired, sprinkle shredded cheese evenly on top.
  6. Bake for 45–50 minutes, or until the top is golden and the center is set.
  7. Let it cool slightly before serving. Enjoy!
