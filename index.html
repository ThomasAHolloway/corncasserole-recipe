Corn Casserole

This easy and delicious corn casserole is a perfect side dish for holidays or family dinners. With just a few ingredients, it comes together in no time and always gets rave reviews!

Ingredients

  • 1 can of whole kernel corn, drained
  • 1 can of creamed corn
  • 1 package of Jiffy corn muffin mix
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, mix together all ingredients until well combined.
  3. Pour mixture into a greased 9x9 inch baking dish.
  4. Bake for 45–50 minutes, or until golden brown on top and set in the middle.
  5. Let rest for 5–10 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
